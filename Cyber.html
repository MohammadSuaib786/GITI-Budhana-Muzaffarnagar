Cyber Safety Awareness

Protecting Your Digital Footprint in the Modern Age

Cyber Safety kya hoti hai?

Cyber Safety ka seedha matlab hai—Satark Rehna. Internet ka istemal karte waqt apni niji jankari (Personal Info), passwords aur digital assets ko hackers aur scammers se bachana hi Cyber Safety hai.

Common Online Threats

  • Phishing Emails & Fraudulent Messages
  • Financial Scams & Impersonation Calls
  • Weak Passwords & Account Takeovers
  • Malicious Websites & Cloned Apps
  • Social Engineering & Identity Theft

Smart Safety Tips

  • Strong Passwords: Mix letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Privacy First: Kabhi bhi OTP ya Password share na karein.
  • Think Before Click: Unknown links par click na karein.
  • Public WiFi: Banking hamesha personal data par karein.
  • 2FA: Two-Factor Authentication har app par on karein.

Emergency: Cyber Crime Report

Agar aapke saath koi fraud hota hai, toh ghabrayein nahi. Yeh steps follow karein:

1. Evidence: Screenshots aur transaction IDs safe rakhein.

2. Contact: National Helpline 1930 par call karein.
